Simple Banana Smoothie

Description

A quick and easy smoothie with only a few ingredients that tastes amazing! I make this with Mott's® fresh-pressed apple juice because it's nice and thick. I also use raw blackberry honey.

Prep:5 min

Total:5 min

Servings:1

Yield:2 cups

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup vanilla yogurt
  • ½ cup apple juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey, or to taste
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 teaspoon milk, or to taste
  • 4 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Blend yogurt, apple juice, honey, bananas, milk and ice cubes in a blender until smooth.
